Pom Pom Drop

Supplies

  • Paper towel rolls
  • Toilet paper rolls
  • Scissors
  • Washi tape
  • Pom poms
  • Other types of small balls (Marbles or bouncy balls)

Steps

  1. Tape the paper towel rolls to the wall or window so the pom pom can roll through them.
  2. You have to cut the rolls in half or openings in the tubes to the ball can travel through.

There is no right answer for this one, it is just to test the concept of gravity and make a rube goldberg machine.

The Science

  Gravity is the force by which a planet or other large body of mass draws objects towards its center.
