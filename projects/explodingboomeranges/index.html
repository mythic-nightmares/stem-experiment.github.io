Exploding Boomeranges

Supplies

  • 4 craft sticks

Steps

  1. Hold one stick in your left hand then place a second craft stick at a 45-55 degree angle.
  2. The third craft stick with line up with the opposite edge of the first craft stick and should land at the halfway point of the second stick.
  3. Using the last craft stick, place it underneath your first craft stick halfway between the two ends and then tuck it underneath the second stick to make a star shape.
